Privacy Policy

Our Policy
We take your privacy seriously and this privacy statement explains how Georgia Graphix collect, use, share and process your information. The policies below are applicable to websites, email newsletters, or any other Web content owned or operated by Georgia Graphix. Georgia Graphix respects each individual user's right to privacy.
We use personal data to help verify accounts and user activity, as well as to promote safety and security, such as by monitoring fraud and investigating suspicious or potentially illegal activity or violations of our terms or policies. Such processing is based on our legitimate interest in helping ensure the safety of our company.
We may collect some personal identifying information from customers while rendering our services but such information will be specifically and knowingly be provided by our customers and we will not use the information without the knowledge of the customer.
We may use this information for marketing purposes if the owner permits us. We occasionally inform our users about our new services and service offers that we believe our users will find interesting. Individual users can request, at any time, that their information not be used for such purposes by opting out of our mailing list(s). We do not rent or sell our list.
