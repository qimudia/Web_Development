Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

You send your request, we work with you on the design till you are satisfied, and you download your design. You can request as many revisions as you like until you are completely satisfied but it takes a longer period to complete.

What can I get designed?

You can get anything designed from your designer. Everything from logo, brochure, website, package design, flyers, t-shirts, trade show graphics and more.

What if I need hundreds of designs?

We have a team for you. No matter how high the volume is you are guaranteed of a high-quality service and your design will be completed on time.

How long does it take to receive designs?

Although we always meet our set goals but please, keep in mind that we value quality over everything else. Our designer will be in constant communication with you and will keep you updated on the status of your designs. Just keep in mind that larger and more complicated designs may take a little longer to finish.

How do you charge for your design?

The cost of design depends on your project or what you want to design. When you send us your request, we will give you a quote.

Can I cancel?

For cancellation, you will have to cancel your designs or graphics within the first 14 days and we will give you a full refund.
